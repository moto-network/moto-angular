why moto?

independence.

your private key controls your assets.
buy, sell or license how you want.
because the structure is decentralized, even if there's no moto; your assets, transactions and contracts still exist on the decentralized web.

transparency.

our tech is open to review.
we make dealing on the decentralized web easier,
but you can do it on your own if you prefer as well.

fairness.

your success is our success.
our fees may range between 1 to 5 percent, depending on the service. and we're always working to make moto better.
